ABOUT

AI CallMate is a smart voice-based communication platform that automates calls, asks AI-generated questions, records responses, and generates insightful reports. Built with Gemini AI, Twilio, and Node.js, it streamlines mass communication for surveys, feedback, and remote interviews. Whether for governments, businesses, or researchers, AI CallMate enables efficient and scalable outreach. It supports dynamic question generation, voice recording, and optional transcription for deeper analysis. Future-ready features include multi-language support, real-time conversations, mobile dashboards, and WhatsApp integration. AI CallMate is your intelligent solution for turning voice interactions into actionable insights—faster, smarter, and at scale.
